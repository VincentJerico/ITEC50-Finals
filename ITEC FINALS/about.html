ABOUT COURSE

ITEC 50 - Web Systems and Technologies is a course that focuses on the principles and technologies used in the development of web-based systems. It is typically offered as part of a curriculum in computer science, information technology, or a related field.

The course covers a range of topics related to web development, including web architecture, client-side and server-side technologies, web programming languages, database integration, and web security. Students gain an understanding of the fundamental concepts and tools used in building interactive and dynamic websites.

Throughout the course; We, the students typically engage in hands-on projects and assignments to apply their knowledge and develop practical skills in web development. By the end of ITEC 50, we should be equipped with the foundational knowledge and skills necessary to design, develop, and deploy web-based systems and technologies.
